About Piemontese Cuisine

Piemonte, the region surrounding Torino, is renowned for its rich culinary tradition. The region is famous for its truffles, wines (including Barolo and Barbaresco), and hearty dishes that reflect both Italian and French influences. This dinner will showcase the best of local gastronomy, providing a true taste of the region's cultural heritage.
